Understanding Addiction

What is addiction?

addiction is a condition where a person cannot stop using a substance or engaging in behaviour.

Causes

stress,environment,peer pressure, and mental health issues contribute to addiction.

How to Overcome

Seek help[,build habits,avoid triggers,and stay connected to support systems.
